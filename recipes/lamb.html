Air Fryer Lamb

Air fryer lamb with soy honey glaze

Ingredients

    Rack of lamb
    Soy Sauce 1 teaspoon
    Oyster Sauce 1 tablespoon
    Honey 2 tablespoon
    Black Vinegar 1 teaspoon
    Garlic 3 cloves
    Cumin Powder
    Cumin Seeds
    Black Pepper
    Cooking Wine
    [Optional] 5 spice powder
    Sale

Steps

  1. Cut lamb into pieces (one bone each or two bones each)
  2. Marinate lamb in salt and cooking wine for 20 mins
  3. Boil lamb in water for 3-5 mins
  4. Pat dry lamb
  5. Wrap foil around bone of lamb
  6. Add cumin powder and black pepper on the lamb
  7. Air fry for 14 mins at 370deg
  8. Mince Garlic
  9. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, and black vinegar
  10. Change proportion of sauce to get desire consistency
  11. Remove lamb from air fryer and apply sauce
  12. Sprinkle Garlic over lamb
  13. Add cumin seeds
  14. Air fry for 5 mins at 400deg
